or

## System requirements

Since LISTA E-Control is a web application, the requirements are simple and minimal:

- **Modern web browser** (Chrome, Firefox, Edge, Safari - latest versions recommended)
- **Screen resolution** of at least 1920x1080 

**Optional:**
- Touchscreen display for interactive operation (optimized for 20 inch touchscreens)
- Barcode scanner
- RFID reader
