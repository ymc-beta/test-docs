## Monitoring low stock products

The ***Low Stock*** screen provides a quick overview of all quantity products that have fallen below their configured warning or alarm thresholds.

### View low stock items

To view the stock level information, navigate to ***`Settings > Low Stock Products`*** (available to all users).

Products are listed with their current stock levels and threshold values. Items can be filtered by level:

- ***Warning*** (yellow) - stock is below warning threshold
- ***Alarm*** (red) - stock is below alarm threshold

Each item links to the product location for quick access to restock.

> [!NOTE]
> Email notifications for low stock events can be configured by an administrator in ***`Settings > Notifications`***.
