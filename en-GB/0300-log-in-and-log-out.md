# Log in and Log out

Every user must log in to access LISTA E-Control. You can log in by entering your username and password on screen, or by scanning a configured RFID chip.

## Log in with Username and Password

1. Enter your login details in the ***Username*** and ***Password*** fields
2. Click the ***Log in*** button or press the ***Enter*** key on your keyboard

## Log in with RFID chip

If an RFID reader is connected to the system:

1. Hold the RFID chip against the RFID reader
2. The system will automatically start the log in process for you

After successful log in, the application opens and displays the default home screen (***Scan screen*** or ***Categories screen***, depending on your configuration).

## First login - password change

If your administrator has enabled mandatory password changes, you will be prompted to set a new password on your first login.

1. Enter your current (temporary) password
2. Enter and confirm your new password
3. Click ***Change Password***

## Log out

> [!WARNING]
> To protect against unauthorized access, always log out when you leave the system 

To log out, click the ***Log out*** button in the navigation bar.

The system will then return to the ***Login screen*** 

## Automatic log out

The system can be configured to automatically log you out after a period of inactivity. This timeout is configured by the administrator in ***`Settings > System settings > General settings`***.
